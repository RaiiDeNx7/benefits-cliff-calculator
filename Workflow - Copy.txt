Child care group = VLOOKUP(location, Location specific data, child care grouping column)
2. NUmber in Household
3. Number of eligible children infant (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"Infant"))
4. Number of eligible children toddler (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"Toddler"))
5. Number of Eligble children 2yo (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"Two year old"))
6. Number of eligble children preschool (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"Preschool"))
7. Number of eligiible children school age (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"School age"))
8 Number of eligible children 13-18 unable to care for themselves (=COUNTIF('Total income package'!$B$48:$B$54,"13-18 years old"))
9. Annual FPL for Household number (=VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A5:C13,2))
10. Monthly FPL for Household number (=VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A5:C13,3))
11. Monthly Income limit for group and HH size (=IF(A3=1,VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A20:F27,3,FALSE),IF(A3=2,VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A20:F27,4,FALSE),IF(A3=3,VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A20:F27,5),IF(A3=4,VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A20:F27,6,FALSE)))))
12. 85% SMI (=VLOOKUP(B2,'Program specific data'!A20:B27,2)



If group = 1 → use Group I limit
If group = 2 → use Group II limit
If group = 3 → use Group III limit
If group = 4 → use Group IV limit

Annual earned income
